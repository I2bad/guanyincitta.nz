About Us

A Journey of Compassion

Welcome to the Guanyin Sanctuary. Since our humble beginnings, we have been dedicated to cultivating a space of peace, wisdom, and selfless service in the heart of the modern world.

Our Mission

Our mission is rooted in the compassionate spirit of Guan Yin Bodhisattva. We aim to provide a sanctuary where all sentient beings can find refuge, learn the timeless wisdom of Buddhism, and discover the path to inner peace. We believe that by nurturing the seeds of enlightenment within ourselves, we can create a ripple effect of kindness across the globe.

Our History

What began in 1988 as a small mountain retreat has grown into a global community of practice. Over the decades, we have expanded our reach from isolated sanctuaries to vibrant urban centers, ensuring that the light of dharma is accessible to everyone, everywhere. Our history is a testament to the enduring power of faith and the collective aspiration for a more mindful world.

Today, we bridge ancient traditions with modern life, using digital platforms and physical spaces to foster a sense of belonging and spiritual growth.
