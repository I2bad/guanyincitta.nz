About Guan Yin Citta Dharma Door关于观音堂 法门

Guan Yin Citta Dharma Door is a highly efficacious Mahayana Buddhist practice transmitted by Guan Yin Bodhisattva to deliver sentient beings in this modern age, suited to the karmic conditions and spiritual capacity of contemporary practitioners.观音堂法门是观世音菩萨传授的大乘佛法修行法门，专为度化现代众生而设，契合当代修行者的因缘与根器，具有殊胜的修行功效。

The Three Golden Practices三大法宝

Guan Yin Citta emphasizes the Three Golden Practices: sutra recitation, making vows, and life liberation (fangsheng). These methods enable practitioners to awaken and help others, freeing themselves from suffering and illness, resolving conflicts and karmic obstacles, and cultivating wisdom and inner peace.观音堂法门以三大法宝为核心：诵经、发愿与放生。这些修行方法能帮助修行者觉悟并利益他人，从痛苦与病患中解脱，化解冤结与业障，培养智慧与内心平静。

Owing to its remarkable efficacy and swift results, Guan Yin Citta has touched the hearts of millions worldwide and continues to grow rapidly.由于其显著的功效与快速的感应，观音堂法门已感动全球数百万众生，并持续快速弘扬。

About Master Jun Hong Lu关于卢军宏老师

Founder of Guan Yin Citta Dharma Door & Renowned Overseas Chinese Leader观音堂法门创始人 & 著名海外华人领袖

Master Jun Hong Lu, the revered founder of Guan Yin Citta Dharma Door and a patriotic leader of the overseas Chinese community, peacefully entered Nirvana on November 10, 2021, at 4:00 a.m. in Sydney, Australia, at the age of 62, having fulfilled his vows in this world.卢军宏老师是观音堂法门的敬仰创始人，也是海外华人社区的爱国领袖。他于2021年11月10日凌晨4时，在澳大利亚悉尼安详圆寂，世寿62岁，此生愿力已满。

Master Lu began his Dharma propagation in 1997 and formally founded Guan Yin Citta Dharma Door in 2006. Over decades of tireless dedication, he compassionately guided countless sentient beings across the globe from confusion and suffering toward the path of enlightenment.卢老师于1997年开始弘法，并于2006年正式创立观音堂法门。数十年来，他不辞辛劳，以慈悲之心引领全球无数众生，从迷惑与苦难中走向觉悟之道。

Master Lu's achievements in propagating Buddhism, preserving Chinese culture, and promoting interfaith harmony gained international recognition. He was repeatedly invited to deliver speeches by UNESCO and was highly respected in global religious and cultural circles.卢老师在弘扬佛法、传承中华文化及促进宗教和谐方面的成就获得了国际社会的广泛认可。他多次应邀在联合国教科文组织发表演讲，在全球宗教与文化界享有崇高声誉。

Our Practice Center我们的修行中心

Guan Yin Citta (NZ) Charitable Trust is the only officially designated Buddhist charitable organization of Guan Yin Citta Dharma Door in New Zealand, registered in Auckland. The trust is committed to promoting Buddhist culture, advancing community welfare, advocating vegetarianism and environmental awareness, and serving both local communities and those with karmic affinity.观音堂（新西兰）慈善信托是观音堂法门在新西兰唯一官方指定的佛教慈善机构，在奥克兰注册成立。本信托致力于弘扬佛教文化、促进社区福祉、倡导素食主义与环保意识，服务本地社区及有缘大众。

Through a wide range of public-interest initiatives, the Trust actively promotes Buddhist values. These include the annual Vegetarian Carnival, as well as regular programs such as community Buddhist study sessions, health and wellness talks, and Chinese language classes for children.通过多种公益活动，本信托积极推广佛教价值观。包括每年举办的素食嘉年华，以及常规项目如社区学佛班、健康讲座和儿童中文课程。

Our Mission我们的使命

Our trust follows the compassionate spirit of Guan Yin Bodhisattva, with a mission to promote true Buddhist teachings, encourage vegetarianism and environmental consciousness, and support the well-being of individuals and communities. By combining cultural education and community outreach, we aim to inspire kindness and build a society rooted in compassion, clarity, and wisdom.我们的信托秉承观世音菩萨的慈悲精神，以弘扬真实佛法、倡导素食与环保意识、支持个人与社区福祉为使命。通过文化教育与社区推广，我们致力于激发善心，构建一个以慈悲、清明与智慧为根基的社会。

We aspire to be a bridge between the wisdom of Buddhism and the broader public, practicing the spirit of the Bodhisattva path, forging wholesome connections, and helping more people attain happiness, clarity, and peace.我们愿成为佛教智慧与广大民众之间的桥梁，践行菩萨道精神，广结善缘，帮助更多人获得幸福、清明与内心平静。
