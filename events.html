Featured Guan Yin Citta Practice Center, Auckland

Buddhism Study Sessions

To cultivate good affinities and promote the wisdom of Buddhism, we regularly hold Buddhist study classes each month in Auckland's East, West, and North Shore regions. These sessions cover Buddha's birthday celebrations, seasonal cultural insights, plain-language Buddhist teachings, and Q&A discussions.
